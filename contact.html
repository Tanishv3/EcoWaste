Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I list my device for sale?

Simply visit our Sell page, fill out the device details form, upload photos, and set your asking price. It's that easy!

Is it safe to sell on EcoWaste?

Yes! We verify all buyers and sellers, provide secure payment processing, and offer buyer/seller protection for peace of mind.

What devices can I sell?

You can sell any electronic device including smartphones, laptops, tablets, gaming consoles, cameras, and more - even if they're broken!

How do I get paid?

Once a sale is confirmed, payment is processed securely through our platform and transferred to your preferred payment method within 1-2 business days.

What if my device doesn't sell?

You can adjust your pricing, improve your listing description, or contact our support team for tips on making your listing more attractive to buyers.

Do you charge any fees?

Listing your device is completely free! We only charge a small commission when your device successfully sells, ensuring our interests are aligned with yours.
